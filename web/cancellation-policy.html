⚠️ Peak Season — Special Rules Apply

For tours departing during Durga Puja, Diwali, Holi, Eid, December 20 – January 5, and other declared long weekends, cancellation charges may be higher than the standard slabs above. These will be clearly communicated in your booking confirmation.

🏢 If We Cancel the Tour

It's rare, but here's our commitment to you

We may need to cancel a tour in exceptional circumstances — such as natural calamities, road closures, political unrest, insufficient group size, or any Force Majeure situation beyond our reasonable control. In such cases, we will:

✅ What we offer you if we cancel:

  • A full credit note valid for 12 months — use it for any future booking
  • OR a refund of the amount paid, minus non-recoverable costs already incurred (hotel deposits, permit fees paid, transport advances, etc.)
  • We'll inform you as early as possible and work with you to find the best alternative

❗ Please note:

If you've booked non-refundable flights, trains, or made personal arrangements for the trip, those losses are not covered by us. This is why we strongly recommend travel insurance — it can cover such situations.

📝 How to Cancel — Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to submit a cancellation

1

Send a written cancellation request

Email us at info@theadventureawaitsco.com or message us on WhatsApp. Include your booking name, travel dates, and destination.

2

We confirm receipt

We'll acknowledge your cancellation within 24 hours and confirm the applicable charge based on the date we received your request.

3

Refund processed (if applicable)

Any eligible refund will be processed within 7–15 working days to your original payment method. You'll receive a confirmation once it's initiated.

4

Consider rescheduling instead!

Before you cancel, ask us about rescheduling. We'd love to help you travel at a better time — often at little to no extra cost.

ℹ️ Important Things to Know

A few more points to keep in mind

Partial usage: If you join a tour and leave midway, no refund is applicable for unused services.

No-shows: If you don't show up on the departure day without prior notice, 100% of the package cost is forfeited — no exceptions.

GST: GST paid at the time of booking is non-refundable as per Indian tax laws, regardless of the reason for cancellation.

Third-party bookings: Hotel or activity bookings made directly by you (outside our package) are governed by the respective provider's policies — we cannot intervene.

Want to reschedule instead?

Talk to us before cancelling — we'll do our best to find a solution!
