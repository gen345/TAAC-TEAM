The Legacy Loop

5 Days / 4 Nights: Old Silk Route Adventure

Your Journey Plan

NJP to NJP | The Historic High-Altitude Circuit

Day 01

Arrival & Riverside Retreat

Pickup from NJP/Bagdogra. Drive to Rishikhola/Rongpokhola. Enjoy the evening by the Rishi River with a riverside campfire and nature walks.

Day 02

Into the Clouds | The Ascent

Permit processing at Rongli. Visit Quekhola Waterfalls. Drive through the dense forests of Nimachen to Padamchen (8,000 ft) for a spectacular sunset.

Day 03

The Legendary Silk Route Trail

Sunrise at Thambi Viewpoint for the Zuluk Zig-Zag Road. Explore Gnathang Valley, Old Baba Mandir, Tukla Valley, and Elephant Lake (Kupup).

Day 04

Lakes & Legends | Aritar

Morning boating at Lampokhari Lake. Visit Aritar Monastery and the historic British Bungalow before a hike to Mankhim Viewpoint.

Day 05

Farewell to the Hills

Final breakfast with Himalayan views. Drive back to NJP/Bagdogra via the Rhenock-Rangpo road, completing the circular loop.
