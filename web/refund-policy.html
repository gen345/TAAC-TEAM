💚 Our Refund Promise

We believe in being honest and fair — always

We understand that cancelling a trip is never easy. When a refund is due, we process it promptly and keep you informed every step of the way. Here's everything you need to know about how refunds work at The Adventure Awaits Co.

🔑 The most important thing to know first:

Refund eligibility depends on when you cancel. The earlier you notify us, the more you get back. Please check our Cancellation Policy to understand the charge slabs before submitting a cancellation request.

⚖️ What's Refundable & What's Not

A clear, honest breakdown

✅ You MAY get a refund for:

  • Balance amount paid (minus applicable cancellation charge)
  • Duplicate payments made by mistake
  • Amount paid over and above package cost
  • Tour cancelled by us (Company) — full amount minus non-recoverable costs
  • Services not delivered as confirmed in writing

❌ You will NOT get a refund for:

  • Advance / token amount (it's non-refundable upon booking)
  • GST paid on the booking (non-refundable per Indian tax law)
  • Services partially used or not availed during the tour
  • No-show on departure day
  • Cancellations within 7 days of departure
  • Personal bookings (flights, trains) made outside our package

🏦 Which account will the refund go to?

Refunds are always returned to the original payment source — the same bank account or UPI ID used to make the payment. We cannot redirect refunds to a different account. If you paid via multiple methods, refunds are split proportionally.

🎟️ Credit Note — A Better Alternative

Sometimes a credit note works out better than a cash refund

If you're not sure when you'll travel next, but don't want to lose the money — ask for a Credit Note instead of a refund. Here's why it can be the smarter choice:

The Adventure Awaits Co. — Credit Note

A credit note lets you hold the value of your booking and use it whenever you're ready to travel again.

  • Valid for 12 months from the date of issue
  • Applicable on any package we offer — same or different destination
  • Can be used for the full amount or part-payment on a new booking
  • No deduction of cancellation charges in most cases
  • Transferable to a family member or friend travelling in your place

💡 When is a Credit Note better?

If you're cancelling due to a sudden health issue, work emergency, or just bad timing — and you plan to travel again within a year — a credit note means you lose nothing compared to a cash refund which may attract cancellation charges. Ask us about it before requesting a cash refund!

🏢 If We Cancel Your Tour

Our commitment when circumstances are beyond our control

In the rare event we have to cancel your tour (due to natural calamity, safety advisories, Force Majeure, etc.), you will receive:

➊  A full Credit Note valid for 12 months — no deductions, no hassles.

➋  OR a refund of the amount paid, minus any non-recoverable costs we've already incurred on your behalf (such as permit fees, hotel deposits, or vehicle advances that vendors won't return).

We'll always be transparent about what was spent and what can be recovered. And we'll do everything we can to rebook your trip at your convenience.

📌 A Few More Important Points

Please read these before raising a refund request

GST is non-refundable. Per Indian tax laws, GST collected at the time of booking cannot be refunded, regardless of the cancellation reason. This applies universally to all tour operators in India.

Partial tour usage. If you join a tour but leave midway — for any reason — there is no refund for services not used. Please plan your trip accordingly.

Disputes about refund amount. If you disagree with our refund calculation, please email us with your concerns. We'll review it and respond within 5 working days. We want every traveller to feel treated fairly.

Refund delays. If 15 working days have passed and you haven't received the refund, please write to us with your UTR/payment reference and we'll investigate immediately.

Questions about your refund?

Write to us and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
