Your Journey Plan

Dibrugarh → Roing → Anini → Tezu → Namsai → Dibrugarh

Day 01

Dibrugarh → Roing | Arrival · Dibang Valley Entry (~105 km / 3–4 hrs)

  • Arrival at Dibrugarh Airport / Railway Station
  • Bhupen Hazarika Setu — drive across India's longest river bridge (9.15 km) over the Lohit
  • ILP Entry Formalities — Arunachal Pradesh Inner Line Permit check at border
  • Sally Lake — serene lake near Roing; great for birdwatching
  • Sunset at Dibang River — golden hour by the pristine Dibang riverbank
🏨 Stay — Roing
💡 Tip: ILP (Inner Line Permit) is mandatory — arrange before travel. Roing is relaxed; use the evening to acclimatize and rest.
Day 02

Roing → Anini | Scenic Mountain Drive · Dibang Valley (~225 km / 7–8 hrs)

  • Mayodia Pass (8,000 ft) — mountain pass with snow in winter; stunning Himalayan views
  • Dibang Valley Drive — breathtaking gorge road through dense forests and towering peaks
  • Waterfall stops en route — multiple cascading waterfalls visible from the road
  • Arrive Anini — remote HQ of Dibang Valley district; one of India's least visited areas
🏨 Stay — Anini
💡 Tip: Very long and demanding mountain drive — start no later than 6 AM. Roads can be challenging post-monsoon. Carry snacks and extra water.
Day 03

Anini · Dri Valley & Border Excursion | Tribal Culture & Indo-China Border

  • Dri Valley — excursion into a stunning pristine valley near the Sino-Indian border
  • Chigu Camp — remote camp amidst pine forest surroundings; rare wilderness experience
  • Mipi — drive towards this remote village near the Indo-China border
  • Mishmi Tribe Interaction — meet the indigenous Mishmi people (subject to availability)
🏨 Stay — Anini
💡 Tip: Some areas near the border may require additional permits. Mishmi tribal interaction is subject to local permissions. Carry snacks for the full day.
Day 04

Anini · Mathu Valley & Emuli Grassland | Valley & Grassland Exploration

  • Mathu Valley — pristine valley with dramatic mountain vistas and dense jungle
  • Emuli Grassland — open rolling grasslands; excellent for birdwatching and landscape photography
  • Leisure evening in Anini — explore the remote town, interact with locals
🏨 Stay — Anini
💡 Tip: Anini has very limited facilities — carry medicines or essentials from Roing or Dibrugarh. Emuli Grassland is best in morning light for photography.
Day 05

Anini → Tezu | Long Drive · Lohit Valley (~275 km / 8–9 hrs)

  • Early departure from Anini after breakfast
  • Scenic drive via Dibang & Lohit Valley — two of Arunachal's great river corridors
  • Tezu District Museum — local tribal artefacts and history (time permitting)
  • Evening by the banks of Lohit River — peaceful riverside relaxation at Tezu
🏨 Stay — Tezu
💡 Tip: Longest drive day — depart by 6 AM. Road conditions on this stretch can be rough. Tezu is beautiful — enjoy the evening by the river.
Day 06

Tezu → Namsai → Dibrugarh | Departure Day · Golden Pagoda (~175 km / 4–5 hrs)

  • Drive to Namsai
  • Golden Pagoda (Kongmu Kham Monastery) — magnificent Theravada Buddhist monastery; one of India's most stunning pagodas
  • Dibru-Saikhowa National Park — brief scenic drive past (subject to time)
  • Drop at Dibrugarh Airport / Railway Station
✈️ Departure — Dibrugarh
💡 Tip: Golden Pagoda is a highlight — allocate at least 1 hour. Reach Dibrugarh well before your departure time.

Before You Go

Travel Tips & Essentials

🌤️ Best Time
October–April. Avoid monsoon (Jun–Sep) — roads to Anini are extremely prone to landslides.
📋 Permits
ILP mandatory for all non-Arunachal residents. Arrange from Guwahati, Dibrugarh, or online before travel.
📶 Connectivity
Extremely limited or no network in Anini and Dri Valley. Download offline maps. Carry a satellite communicator if possible.
🏕️ Remoteness
Anini is one of India's most remote district HQs. Facilities are very basic — carry medicines, snacks, power banks, and warm clothing.
🎒 Gear
Warm layers, waterproof jacket, trekking footwear, headlamp, personal first aid kit, and a refillable water bottle are essential.
💵 Cash
Carry sufficient cash from Dibrugarh. No ATMs in Anini and very limited facilities in Tezu.
