5 Days / 4 Nights — NJP to NJP

HimalayanEscape

Your Journey Plan

NJP to NJP | Detailed Circular Experience

Day 01

NJP to Lepchajagat | The Forest Retreat

Transition from the city into profound mountain silence. Enjoy a gentle walk through towering pines and conclude your day with a breathtaking sunset over the Kanchenjunga range.

Day 02

Transition to Darjeeling | Queen of Hills

Shift from forest trails to the vibrant energy of Darjeeling. Visit the HMI, Zoological Park, and the Japanese Temple. Spend your evening at the historic Chowrasta (Mall Road).

Day 03

Tiger Hill Sunrise | Takdah & Tinchuley

Witness the 4:00 AM sunrise at Tiger Hill and the Batasia Loop. Later, explore colonial heritage bungalows in Takdah and capture the golden hour at Gumbadara Viewpoint.

Day 04

Ahaldara Viewpoint | Sittong Orchards

Experience a 360-degree panoramic view of the Himalayas and Teesta River from Ahaldara. Wander through the world-famous Orange Orchards of Sittong and visit Jogighat Bridge.

Day 05

Mirik Lake | Return to NJP

Drive via Simana along the Nepal Border. Enjoy peaceful boating on Mirik Lake before your final descent to NJP Station/Bagdogra Airport for departure.
