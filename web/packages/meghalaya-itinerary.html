Your Journey Plan

Guwahati to Guwahati | 2N Shillong + 2N Cherrapunji

Day 01

Guwahati → Shillong | Arrival + City Sightseeing

  • Pickup from Guwahati Airport / Railway Station
  • Umiam Lake (Barapani) — En-route scenic stop; 30–40 min lakeside break
  • Hotel Check-in + Lunch — Settle in before afternoon sightseeing
  • Don Bosco Museum — 7-floor cultural museum of Northeast India
  • Ward's Lake — Colonial-era boating lake in central Shillong
  • Drive past Shillong Golf Course — One of Asia's oldest scenic courses
  • Evening Walk at Police Bazaar — Local street food, markets & city vibe
🏨 Stay — Shillong
💡 Tip: Drive is approx 3–4 hrs. Umiam Lake stop helps break the journey. Keep afternoon light for guests.
Day 02

Shillong Local + Laitlum | Peaks, Canyons & City

  • Shillong Peak — Highest point in Meghalaya; panoramic views (permit dependent)
  • Elephant Falls — Beautiful 3-tiered waterfall just outside Shillong
  • Laitlum Canyon — Dramatic 'End of Hills' gorge; jaw-dropping depth & valley views
  • Return to Shillong by evening
🏨 Stay — Shillong
💡 Tip: Check Shillong Peak permit availability the evening before. Laitlum is best in the afternoon when mist clears.
Day 03

Shillong → Cherrapunji | Sohra Waterfall Circuit

  • Mawkdok Valley View Point — Stunning gorge viewpoint on the Sohra highway
  • Nohkalikai Falls — India's tallest plunge waterfall (340m); best in morning light
  • Seven Sisters Falls — Iconic 7-segmented seasonal waterfall
  • Mawsmai Cave — Illuminated limestone cave; easy family-friendly walk-through
  • Arwah Cave — Fossil-rich cave with natural skylights (optional)
  • Eco Park Sohra — Viewpoint and recreation area (optional)
  • Check-in at Cherrapunji hotel · Cliff sunset from stay
🏨 Stay — Cherrapunji (Sohra)
💡 Tip: Early start is key — long day with multiple stops. Pack lunch or plan a quick roadside meal.
Day 04

Cherrapunji → Dawki → Mawlynnong → Cherrapunji

  • Drive to Dawki — Approx 2 hrs from Cherrapunji via scenic hill roads
  • Boating at Umngot River, Dawki — Crystal-clear glass-water river boating
  • Indo-Bangladesh Border Viewpoint — View across the international boundary
  • Mawlynnong — Asia's cleanest village (2003); bamboo walkways & eco-tourism
  • Single Deck Living Root Bridge — Natural root bridge near Mawlynnong
  • Return to Cherrapunji by late evening
🏨 Stay — Mawlynnong/Dawki
💡 Tip: Very early start (6:30–7 AM) is essential. Book boating in advance during peak season.
Day 05

Cherrapunji → Guwahati | Departure Day

  • Relaxed breakfast at Cherrapunji hotel
  • Optional stop — Wei Sawdong Falls or Garden of Caves if time allows
  • Drive to Guwahati via Shillong (approx 4–5 hrs total)
  • Optional quick café stop in Shillong if time permits
  • Drop at Guwahati Airport / Railway Station
✈️ Departure — Guwahati
💡 Tip: Cherrapunji–Guwahati is approx 4–5 hrs. Depart by 8 AM to be comfortable for afternoon flights or trains.

Before You Go

Travel Tips & Essentials

🌤️ Best Time
October – May. Monsoon (Jun–Sep) is scenic but roads can be challenging.
🏨 Stays
2 nights Shillong · 2 nights Cherrapunji. Dawki & Mawlynnong as a day trip.
📶 Connectivity
Download offline maps. Signal weak in Mawsynram, Laitlum & Mawlynnong.
📋 Permits
Shillong Peak needs a permit — confirm availability the evening before.
🎒 Gear
Comfortable walking shoes, light rain jacket, torch & refillable water bottle.
