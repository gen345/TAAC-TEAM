Your Journey Plan

NJP → Rinchenpong → Pelling → Ravangla → Namchi → NJP

Day 01

NJP / Bagdogra → Rinchenpong | Arrival & Local Exploration

  • Warm reception at NJP Railway Station / Bagdogra Airport
  • Proceed through the scenic Teesta Valley to Rinchenpong
  • Rinchenpong Monastery — serene hilltop monastery with valley views
  • Bikh Pokhri — sacred lake with mystical surroundings
  • Heritage British Bungalow ruins — colonial-era heritage exploration
  • Kanchenjunga Viewpoints — panoramic views of the mighty Kanchenjunga range
🏨 Stay — Rinchenpong
💡 Tip: Rinchenpong is a hidden gem — quiet, offbeat and scenic. Best Kanchenjunga views are at sunrise from the village.
Day 02

Rinchenpong → Pelling via Dentam & Singshore (~60 km / 4 hrs)

  • After breakfast, proceed towards Pelling via a scenic offbeat route
  • Dentam Valley — lush green valley with cardamom farms and dense forests
  • Singshore Bridge — highest suspension bridge in Sikkim with breathtaking gorge views
  • Panoramic valley views throughout the journey
  • Arrive Pelling — check-in and evening leisure
🏨 Stay — Pelling
💡 Tip: Singshore Bridge is a highlight — stop for photos. The Dentam route is less travelled and beautifully scenic.
Day 03

Full Day Pelling Sightseeing

  • Sky Walk Pelling — glass skywalk with stunning Kanchenjunga views
  • Chenrezig Statue — massive Buddha statue overlooking the valley
  • Pemayangtse Monastery — one of Sikkim's oldest and most revered monasteries
  • Rabdentse Ruins — ancient ruins of Sikkim's second royal capital
  • Khecheopalri Lake — sacred wish-fulfilling lake sacred to Buddhists & Hindus
  • Kanchenjunga Falls & Rimbi Waterfalls — stunning cascades in the forest
  • Local Orange Garden — Sikkim's famous orange orchards
🏨 Stay — Pelling
💡 Tip: This is a packed day — start by 8 AM. Khecheopalri Lake requires a short walk; carry comfortable shoes.
Day 04

Pelling → Ravangla & Buddha Park (~65 km / 4 hrs)

  • After breakfast, proceed to Ravangla
  • En route — scenic viewpoints, monasteries & forest drives
  • After check-in & lunch, visit Buddha Park — giant Buddha statue with beautifully landscaped gardens
🏨 Stay — Ravangla
💡 Tip: Ravangla offers stunning Kanchenjunga sunrise views. Set your alarm early for Day 5 morning.
Day 05

Ravangla Sightseeing & Transfer to Namchi

  • Ralong Monastery — beautiful monastery with mountain backdrop
  • Titanic View Point — panoramic Himalayan views
  • Temi Tea Garden — Sikkim's only tea estate; scenic drive through the estate
  • Samdruptse Hill — massive Guru Padmasambhava statue on the hilltop
  • Proceed to Namchi — check-in at hotel
  • Char Dham Namchi — replica of India's four holy dhams in one complex
🏨 Stay — Namchi
💡 Tip: Char Dham is best visited in the evening — beautifully lit after sunset.
Day 06

Namchi → NJP / Bagdogra via Tarey Bhir (~120 km / 5 hrs)

  • After breakfast, proceed for the return journey
  • Tarey Bhir — breathtaking cliffside viewpoint overlooking the Teesta Valley
  • Drop at NJP Railway Station / Bagdogra Airport with cherished memories
✈️ Departure — NJP / Bagdogra
💡 Tip: Plan your onward train or flight after 4 PM — the drive takes ~5 hrs from Namchi.

Before You Go

Travel Tips & Essentials

🌤️ Best Time
March–May (spring blooms, rhododendrons) and October–December (clear skies, Kanchenjunga views). Avoid monsoon June–Sept.
📋 Permits
Carry original Aadhaar / Voter ID. Some areas may require permits — we assist with all documentation.
🥾 Footwear
Comfortable walking shoes needed for Khecheopalri Lake, Rabdentse Ruins and Tarey Bhir viewpoint walks.
💵 Cash
Carry sufficient cash from NJP / Siliguri. ATMs are available in Pelling and Namchi but scarce in smaller villages.
📶 Connectivity
Mobile signal is good in Pelling, Ravangla and Namchi. Rinchenpong may have limited signal.
🧥 Clothing
Light woolens for evenings. Mornings can be cold at Pelling and Rinchenpong. Carry a light rain jacket year-round.
