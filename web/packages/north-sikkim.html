Your Journey Plan

NJP to NJP | The High-Altitude Alpine Circuit

Day 01

Arrival & Capital Vibes

Pickup from NJP/Bagdogra. Drive to Gangtok. Check-in at the hotel and spend your evening exploring MG Marg, the cultural heart of the city.

Day 02

The High Frontier | Lachen

Drive to Lachen (8,800 ft). Witness the Seven Sisters Waterfalls and Singhik View Point for a panoramic view of Mt. Kanchenjunga.

Day 03

Sacred Waters & Lachung

Early morning excursion to Gurudongmar Lake (17,800 ft). Traverse through Thangu and Chopta Valley's alpine meadows before heading to Lachung.

Day 04

Valley of Flowers | Yumthang

Explore Yumthang Valley and its natural Hot Springs. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, this is Sikkim's "Valley of Flowers." Return to Gangtok by evening.

Day 05

Farewell to the Mountains

Final breakfast in Gangtok. Drive back to NJP/Bagdogra with memories of the high Himalayas.
