Your Journey Plan

NJP → Rishikhola → Padamchen → Nathang → Zuluk → Sillery Gaon → NJP

Day 01

NJP → Rishikhola / Aritar | Arrival & Riverside Retreat

  • Pickup from NJP Railway Station / Bagdogra Airport
  • Drive through the scenic Teesta Valley
  • Arrive Rishikhola / Aritar (~2,000 ft) — lush green, warm and relaxing
  • Evening by the banks of the Rishi River — bonfire and riverside walk
🏨 Stay — Rishikhola / Aritar
💡 Vibe: Lush green, warm, and relaxing — the perfect Himalayan welcome.
Day 02

Rishikhola → Padamchen | The Gateway to Sikkim

  • Aritar (Lampokhari) Lake — boating at one of Sikkim's oldest natural lakes
  • Mankhim Viewpoint — panoramic bird's eye view of the lake
  • Rongli Dam & Permit Office — official entry point and permit processing
  • Que Khola Falls — beautiful roadside waterfall stop
  • Arrive Padamchen (~8,000 ft) — stunning sunset valley views
🏨 Stay — Padamchen
💡 Tip: Carry original Aadhaar / Voter ID — permit processing at Rongli is mandatory for the Silk Route.
Day 03

The High Altitude Loop — The "BIG" Day

  • Thambi Viewpoint — view of the iconic 32-turn Zuluk Zig-Zag Road
  • Lungthung & Gnathang Valley (~9,400 ft) — high-altitude plateau, often snow-capped
  • Old Baba Mandir — original shrine of Sepoy Harbhajan Singh
  • Elephant Lake / Kupup — unique lake shaped like an elephant
  • Tukla Valley — historic 1904 British-Tibet battle site
🏨 Stay — Agamlok / Zuluk
💡 Tip: This is the highlight day — start very early. Carry heavy woolens; Nathang Valley is freezing even in summer.
Day 04

Agamlok/Zuluk → Sillery Gaon | The Descent to Bengal

  • Scenic drive through Kalimpong's pine forests
  • Ramitey Viewpoint — famous for the 14 bends of the Teesta River
  • Damsang Fort Ruins — optional short nature trek
  • Arrive Sillery Gaon (~6,000 ft) — the "New Darjeeling" experience
🏨 Stay — Sillery Gaon
💡 Tip: Sillery Gaon offers stunning Kanchenjunga views at sunrise — set your alarm early!
Day 05

Sillery Gaon → NJP / IXB | Final Descent & Departure

  • Morning views of Mt. Kanchenjunga from the balcony
  • Drive through Cinchona and tea plantations
  • Coronation Bridge, Sevoke — final photo stop at the Teesta bridge
  • Drop-off at NJP by 3:00 PM – 4:00 PM
✈️ Departure — NJP / IXB
💡 Tip: Plan your onward train or flight after 5 PM to be comfortable. The drive takes ~3–4 hrs from Sillery Gaon.

Before You Go

Travel Tips & Essentials

📋 Documents
Carry 4 passport-size photos and original ID. Voter ID is preferred at Rongli permit office.
💊 Health
Carry motion sickness and altitude meds. Zig-Zag roads are steep — avoid heavy meals before the drive.
🧥 Clothing
Heavy woolens required for Day 3 — Nathang Valley is freezing even in summer. Light layers for lower altitudes.
🌤️ Best Time
March–May (spring) and October–December (clear skies). Avoid monsoon June–Sept — landslides are common.
💵 Cash
Carry sufficient cash from NJP / Siliguri. ATMs are unavailable beyond Rongli on the Silk Route.
📶 Connectivity
Mobile signal is very limited in Zuluk, Gnathang and Agamlok. Download offline maps before departure.
