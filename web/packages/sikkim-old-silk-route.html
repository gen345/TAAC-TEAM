Your Journey Plan

NJP / IXB → Rishikhola → Padamchen → Zuluk → Aritar → NJP / IXB

Day 01

NJP / IXB → Rishikhola | Arrival & Riverside Retreat

  • Pickup from NJP Railway Station / Bagdogra Airport
  • Scenic drive to Rishikhola / Rongpokhola (~4 hrs)
  • Check-in at riverside homestay and freshen up
  • Evening by the Rishi River — campfire, nature walks & mountain air
🏨 Stay — Rishikhola
💡 Tip: Rishikhola is a beautiful entry into Sikkim. Relax by the river — the journey starts here.
Day 02

Rishikhola → Padamchen | Into the Clouds

  • Permit processing at Rongli — ILP for Silk Route
  • Quekhola Waterfalls — scenic stop en route
  • Drive through dense forests of Nimachen
  • Arrive Padamchen (8,000 ft) — spectacular sunset over the valley
🏨 Stay — Padamchen
💡 Tip: Carry your Aadhaar / Voter ID — permits are checked at Rongli checkpoint. Original ID mandatory.
Day 03

The Legendary Silk Route Trail | Zuluk & Gnathang

  • Thambi Viewpoint — sunrise over the iconic Zuluk Zig-Zag Road with 32 hairpin bends
  • Gnathang Valley (13,000 ft) — vast alpine meadows & army camp views
  • Old Baba Mandir & Tukla Valley — sacred army shrine and memorial
  • Elephant Lake / Kupup (13,066 ft) — high-altitude lake near the Indo-China border
🏨 Stay — Zuluk
💡 Tip: Start very early for Thambi sunrise. Carry warm layers — temperatures drop sharply at Gnathang.
Day 04

Zuluk → Aritar | Lakes & Legends

  • Morning boating at Lampokhari Lake — Sikkim's oldest natural lake
  • Aritar Monastery — peaceful Buddhist monastery with valley views
  • Historic British Bungalow — colonial-era heritage property at Aritar
  • Hike to Mankhim Viewpoint — panoramic Kanchenjunga range views
🏨 Stay — Aritar
💡 Tip: Mankhim hike is about 1.5 km — wear comfortable shoes. Best views in the morning before clouds arrive.
Day 05

Aritar → NJP / IXB | Farewell to the Hills

  • Final breakfast with Himalayan views
  • Drive back to NJP / Bagdogra via the Rhenock–Rangpo road
  • Completing the circular Legacy Loop — tour ends here
✈️ Departure — NJP / IXB
💡 Tip: Plan your onward train or flight with sufficient buffer. Drive takes ~4 hrs during peak season.

Before You Go

Travel Tips & Essentials

🌤️ Best Time
March–May (spring blooms) and October–December (clear skies). Avoid monsoon June–Sept — roads get dangerous.
📋 Permits
Inner Line Permit (ILP) required for the Silk Route. We arrange this — carry original Aadhaar / Voter ID. Foreigners need a Restricted Area Permit.
🏔️ Altitude
Gnathang Valley and Kupup Lake are at 13,000+ ft. Carry warm clothing even in summer. Acclimatise gradually.
💵 Cash
Carry sufficient cash from Siliguri / NJP. ATMs are scarce beyond Rongli on the Silk Route.
📶 Connectivity
Mobile signal is patchy in Zuluk, Gnathang and Padamchen. Download offline maps before departure.
🎒 Gear
Heavy woolens, windproof jacket, comfortable trekking shoes and a rain jacket are essential year-round.
