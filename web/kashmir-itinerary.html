6 Days / 5 Nights: The Crown of Kashmir

Your Journey Plan

Jammu to Jammu | Detailed Experience

Day 01

Arrival in Jammu | Transfer to Srinagar

Meet our representative at Jammu and proceed towards Srinagar. Enjoy a Stay at cozy Houseboat with Complimentary 1 Hr Sikara Ride in a traditional hand-carved Houseboat on Dal Lake. Relax on the deck and in a traditional hand-carved Houseboat on Dal Lake. Relax on the deck and enjoy the serene floating life.

Day 02

Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Transfer to your hotel. Visit the famous Mughal Gardens: Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh. Later, visit the historic Shankaracharya Temple for a panoramic view of the city.

Day 03

Gulmarg – The Meadow of Flowers

Full-day excursion to Gulmarg. Explore high-altitude meadows and pine forests. Optional Gondola Cable Car ride to reach the snow peaks of Apharwat.

Day 04

Sonamarg – The Meadow of Gold

Full-day trip to Sonamarg along the Sindh River. Visit the Thajiwas Glacier by hiring a local pony or trekking to enjoy the alpine scenery.

Day 05

Srinagar to Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam, stopping at Saffron Fields and Awantipora Ruins. Explore the ABC Valleys (Aru, Betaab, and Chandanwari) via local Union Taxi.

Day 06

Baisaran Valley | Return to Jammu

Experience a Pony Ride to Baisaran Valley (Mini Switzerland). Begin the return journey through the scenic mountain highway for drop-off at Jammu.
