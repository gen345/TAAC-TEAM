6 Days / 5 Nights: The Crown of Kashmir

Your Journey Plan

Jammu to Jammu | Detailed Experience

Day 01

Arrival in Jammu | Transfer to Srinagar

Meet our representative at Jammu and proceed towards Srinagar. Enjoy a Stay at cozy Houseboat with Complimentary 1 Hr Shikara Ride on Dal Lake.

Day 02

Srinagar Local Sightseeing

Visit the famous Mughal Gardens: Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh. Later, visit the historic Shankaracharya Temple for a panoramic view.

Day 03

Gulmarg – The Meadow of Flowers

Full-day excursion to Gulmarg. Explore high-altitude meadows and pine forests. Optional Gondola Cable Car ride to Apharwat peaks.

Day 04

Sonamarg – The Meadow of Gold

Full-day trip to Sonamarg along the Sindh River. Visit the Thajiwas Glacier by hiring a local pony or trekking.

Day 05

Srinagar to Pahalgam

Drive to Pahalgam, stopping at Saffron Fields. Explore the ABC Valleys (Aru, Betaab, and Chandanwari) via local Union Taxi.

Day 06

Baisaran Valley | Return to Jammu

Experience a Pony Ride to Baisaran Valley (Mini Switzerland). Begin the return journey for drop-off at Jammu.
